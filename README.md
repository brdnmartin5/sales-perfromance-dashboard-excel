# sales-excel-dashboard

## Objective
Analyze sales data to identify key trends across regions, product categories and time.

##Tools Used
- Microsoft Excel (Pivot Tables, Charts, Dashboard Design)

## Key Insights
- The West region generated the hightest total sales, followed by the East region.
- Technology is the top-performing category, outperforming Furniture and Office Supplies.
- Sales show an overall upward trend throughout the year, with a noticeable dip mid year and strong growth toward the end of the year.

## Business Recommendations
- Continue investing in high performing regions like the West and East.
- Focus on expanding the Technology categpry, as it drives the most revenue.
- Investigate mid year drips in sales to identify potential operational or seasonal issues.
